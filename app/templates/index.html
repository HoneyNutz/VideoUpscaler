Getting Started

  1. Select a Menu Item: Use the sidebar to navigate between sections like Video Upscaler, Batch Processing, and Storage Management.
  2. Select a Video: In the 'Video Upscaler' section, drag & drop a video or click the upload area to choose a file. The selected filename will be displayed.
  3. Choose Settings: Select your desired 'Upscale Factor' and 'AI Model'. Model descriptions are provided to help you choose.
  4. Process: Click the Process Video button to start. Monitor the progress bar and status updates.
  5. (Optional) Set Save Location after Processing: In the success screen, choose a server folder where the enhanced video should be saved. If left empty, the video will be downloaded to your device.
  6. Save Your Video: In the success screen, either save to the chosen folder or download directly.

AI Model Guide

  • RealESRGAN x4+ (General): The best all-around model for most content, including photos and real-world videos. Excellent for 3GP mobile videos.
  • General v3 (Balanced): A well-balanced model offering a good mix of quality and performance.
  • RealESRNet x4+ (Detail): Use this for videos where preserving fine details and textures is critical.
  • RealESRGAN x2+ (Fast): A 2x upscaler that is significantly faster than 4x models. Ideal for quick enhancements or very large videos.
  • Anime Video v3: Specifically trained for anime and cartoons. Provides great results with temporal consistency (less flickering).
  • Anime Compact: A smaller, faster version for anime. Good for quick processing of animated content.

Features Explained

Batch Processing
Process an entire folder of videos at once. Select the folder, choose your settings, and the app will process all supported video files inside it.
Storage Management
View your disk usage for uploaded and processed files. Use the cleanup buttons to clear space by deleting files from the server and clearing database records.
3GP Optimization
Videos in 3GP or 3G2 format are automatically converted to MP4 after upscaling, ensuring broad compatibility with modern devices and players.

Troubleshooting

  • Processing Fails: Ensure the video format is supported (MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, 3GP, 3G2). Very long or high-resolution videos may require more system resources. Try a smaller video or the 2x model.
  • File Too Large: The maximum upload size is 2GB. For larger files, consider using the command-line interface.
  • Slow Performance: Upscaling is resource-intensive. Performance depends on your hardware (especially GPU). The 2x model is faster than 4x models.
  • Storage Cleanup Not Working: If the buttons don't seem to work, try reloading the page. If the issue persists, there may be a backend server issue.
